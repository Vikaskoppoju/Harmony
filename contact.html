Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an interior design project take? +

Timelines vary by project scope. A single room redesign typically takes 4–8 weeks, while a full villa or commercial space can range from 3–12 months. We provide a detailed timeline after the initial consultation and site assessment.

Do you work with clients outside of your office cities? +

Absolutely. We regularly take on projects across the GCC and India. Our team travels to client sites for key milestones, and we use detailed 3D visualizations and virtual walkthroughs to keep remote projects on track.

What is included in the initial consultation? +

Our initial consultation is a 60-minute session where we discuss your vision, lifestyle, budget, and timeline. We conduct a site survey, present mood boards, and outline a customized project proposal. The first consultation is complimentary.

Can you handle both design and construction? +

Yes — this is one of our key strengths. Our integrated design-build model means one team handles everything from concept drawings and material selection through to construction, fit-out, and final styling. This reduces cost, delays, and miscommunication.

What design styles do you specialize in? +

We are proficient in Contemporary, Modern Luxury, Arabian/Middle Eastern, Minimalist, Biophilic, Classic European, and transitional styles. More importantly, we specialize in creating bespoke designs that reflect each client's individual personality.

How do you handle project budgets? +

We work transparently with fixed-price contracts for defined scopes. After the initial brief, we provide a detailed cost breakdown. We also offer value engineering options to help clients achieve maximum impact within their budget.

Do you offer 3D visualizations before work begins? +

Yes, photorealistic 3D renderings and virtual walkthroughs are a standard part of our process. You will see exactly how your space will look — including materials, lighting, and furniture placement — before a single nail is hammered.

Is there a minimum project size? +

We welcome projects of all sizes — from a single room makeover to a full estate. For smaller consultations, we offer hourly advisory sessions. Contact us and we'll recommend the engagement model that best suits your needs.
