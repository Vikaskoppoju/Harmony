Our Story

Crafting Harmony
in Every Space

Founded in 2022, Harmony Spaces by G began as a boutique interior design studio with a single mission — to make extraordinary design accessible.

What started as a small practice in Hyderabad has grown into a pan-regional powerhouse with offices across Bahrain, Abu Dhabi, Oman, Hyderabad, and Bangalore. Today, we serve discerning clients across the Middle East and India, delivering end-to-end design and construction solutions that stand the test of time.

Our multidisciplinary team of over 45 professionals — architects, interior designers, project managers, and master craftsmen — collaborate on every project to ensure no detail is overlooked and every vision is exceeded.

Chief Executive Officer Ganesh Reddy Velagala
Visionary Leader

Ganesh Reddy Velagala

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 5 years of experience spanning luxury residential design, high-end commercial interiors, and large-scale construction across the GCC and India, Ganesh Reddy Velagala is the creative and strategic force behind Harmony Spaces by G.

Born with an innate passion for architecture and beauty, Ganesh founded Harmony Spaces in 2022 with a conviction that great design should be deeply personal — a true reflection of the people who inhabit the space. Under his leadership, the firm has grown rapidly across five countries and earned a reputation for delivering spaces that are as functional as they are extraordinary.

Ganesh holds a strong foundation in design and construction management and brings a culturally rich perspective to every project — bridging the aesthetics of the Indian subcontinent with the refined luxury demands of the Middle East. He is a passionate advocate for quality craftsmanship and sustainable, people-first design.

Founded in Bahrain

Harmony Spaces by G opens its first studio in Bahrain with a passionate team and a bold vision for luxury interiors across the Gulf.

Expanded into Abu Dhabi and Muscat, bringing our signature design ethos to two of the Gulf's most thriving luxury markets.

Established our India headquarters in Hyderabad, bridging premium design between the GCC and South Asia's booming real estate market.
